Milkrice Recipe

Ingredients

  • Coconut
  • Rice
  • Salt

Method

  1. Step 1: To prepare milk rice, start by rinsing 1 cup of rice thoroughly. In a pot, combine the rice with 4 cups of milk, adding a pinch of salt.
  2. Step 2: Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer, stirring occasionally.
  3. Step 3: As the rice cooks and the mixture thickens, add sugar to taste and continue cooking until the milk rice achieves a creamy consistency.
  4. Step 4: Optionally, enhance the flavor with cardamom or vanilla. Serve the warm milk rice, and consider garnishing with nuts or fruit for an extra touch of deliciousness.
  5. Step 5: Adjust the sugar and flavorings according to your taste preferences, and savor the homemade goodness.
