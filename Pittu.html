Pittu Recipe

Ingredients

  • Coconut
  • Rice Flour
  • Salt

Method

  1. Step 1: To prepare pittu, begin by combining 2 cups of roasted rice flour with 1 cup of grated coconut in a mixing bowl.
  2. Step 2: Gradually add water, aiming for a crumbly texture, and incorporate 1 teaspoon of salt.
  3. Step 3: Fill the pittu steamer with the mixture, layering with grated coconut if desired.
  4. Step 4: Steam for 15-20 minutes until firm.
  5. Step 5: Once done, gently loosen the steamed pittu and serve it warm with accompaniments like curries or coconut milk, savoring the essence of this traditional South Asian dish.
