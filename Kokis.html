Kokis Recipe

Ingredients

  • Rice Flour
  • Tumeric
  • Coconut
  • Curry Leaves

Method

  1. Step 1: The batter, a harmonious blend of rice flour, all-purpose flour, coconut milk, and a hint of turmeric for color, is transformed into delicate, crispy shapes through a traditional kokis mold.
  2. Step 2: Deep-fried to a golden perfection, these intricately formed treats absorb the richness of coconut and possess a satisfying crunch.
  3. Step 3: Enjoyed cooled, kokis adds a delightful crispiness to celebratory moments, making it a beloved part of Sri Lankan culinary traditions.
