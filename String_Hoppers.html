String Hoppers Recipe

Ingredients

  • Rice Flour
  • Hot Water
  • Salt

Method

  1. Step 1: To prepare these delicate noodles, a dough of rice flour and salt is meticulously crafted and pressed through small holes onto circular trays, forming intricate strands.
  2. Step 2: Steam transforms these creations into tender, yet resilient, string hoppers.
  3. Step 3: Served with an array of accompaniments such as curries or coconut chutney, they absorb the essence of the accompanying flavors, offering a versatile and delightful culinary experience that captures the essence of South Asian gastronomy.
